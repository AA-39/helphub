Empower UTH

Charity Profile






Igniting Dreams, Transforming Futures.

EmpowerUth stands as a beacon of hope for underprivileged youth, dedicated to breaking the chains of poverty through education, mentorship, and skill development. Our charity envisions a future where every young individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to opportunities for personal and academic growth. By offering tutoring, mentorship, and organizing various skill-building programs, EmpowerUth aims to empower the youth, providing them with the tools and support needed to shape their own destinies. Join us in the mission of igniting dreams and transforming futures.




Volunteers Responsibilities

  • Tutoring and mentoring disadvantaged youth.
  • Organizing career guidance workshops.
  • Assisting in fundraising events for educational resources.
  • Leading extracurricular activities for youth engagement.

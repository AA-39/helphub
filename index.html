Welcome to HelpHub™


Helping Heroes Find Other Heroes





Who Are We?

At HelpHub™, our goal is to connect
every-day heroes like yourself
with charities in desperate
need of hands to help
fulfill their noble missions.
We hope to connect as
many humanitarian heroes
together as we possibly
can for the betterment of
humanity, and aim to keep expanding
our circle of charities
for the forseeable future.
We thank you for
visiting our website,
and we hope you find a charity
you can partner with
to make a difference in our world.

What Do We Do?

We at HelpHub™ will act as your
middleman in your search for a
charity to volunteer with.
We are currently
partnered with four
different charities.
You can find our list
of wonderful charities
by going to our "Charities" page,
which you can find at the
top of our website.
From there you can read
all about these wonderful
organizations by clicking
on their Charity Profile link!
If you like what you see,
you could even send them
an email and ask to volunteer!

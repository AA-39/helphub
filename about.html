HelpHub™ was founded in 2022 by two college students, Amanda Ackerman and Jerry Caviston. The two had spent their early years always wanting to do more volunteer work for their communities, but never knew exactly how to start. The two decided to solve this problem for themselves and the other members of their community. Together, the two decided to create a platform for everyday heroes who want to help their communities so they could find charities who need their help. HelpHub™ was born from the hearts of these two people, but continues to grow and expand. As more charities join us, and more volunteers like you contact those charities, we can begin to truly make a difference together.
